Why We Started Vehdic

In a world flooded with synthetic products, we asked: what would our ancestors use? The answer was always rooted in nature — the cow, the herbs, the fire.

Vehdic is our commitment to preserving these ancient practices. Every product we make is a bridge between Vedic wisdom and modern life — making it simple, accessible and genuinely effective.

We source exclusively from Gir cow farms in Gujarat, work with Ayurvedic practitioners, and never compromise on purity. This is not just a business — it is seva (service) to the ancient knowledge that still heals.
